## Suggested Learning Path

Build foundations before risking real money.

**Step 1: Read complete Knowledge Base** (2-3 hours)

**Step 2: Configure MECHA-X indicator** (30 mins)

**Step 3: Study Chart Examples gallery** (1-2 hours)

**Step 4: Mark up 20 historical setups** (3-4 hours)

**Step 5: Paper trade 50 setups** (2-4 weeks)

**Step 6: Go live with micro contracts** (1-2 months)

**Step 7: Scale up as consistency builds** (Ongoing)
