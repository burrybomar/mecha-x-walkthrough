## Trade Entry Form

When adding a new trade, the following fields are captured:

### Basic Information
- **Date:** [Date picker]
- **Pair/Asset:** ES, NQ, EUR/USD, etc.
- **Direction:** Long / Short
- **Outcome:** Pending / Win / Loss / Breakeven

### Setup Details
- **HTF Bias:** Daily bullish, 4H in discount...
- **Sweep Type:** SSL sweep at 4900, BSL at 15500...

### Trade Execution
- **Entry:** Price level
- **Stop:** Stop loss level
- **Target:** Target price
- **R:R:** Risk/Reward ratio (e.g., 1:3)

### Notes
- **Notes:** What worked? What didn't? Session timing? C2 quality?

## Journal Purpose

This trade journal helps you:
- Track your setups systematically
- Review what works and what doesn't
- Maintain accountability
- Build pattern recognition over time
- Analyze your trading performance

Remember: Consistent journaling builds consistency in trading.
